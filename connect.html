What do you need to start mining ERGO?




  • 1. Ergo wallet address to receive your rewards.
  • 2. A mining GPU sometimes called Video or Graphics card on your computer. Your GPU should have at least 4GB of ram.
  • 3. The mining software capable of mining ERGO.




  • Get your ERGO Wallet

  • There are 2 wallets you can use. I will recommend the yoroi wallet for easier setup.
  • ERGO Full node wallet. For experienced users. ERGO FULL NODE SETUP
  • ERGO Yoroi Wallet. For easier setup. YOROI WALLET
  • Download the wallet using the above links and get your wallet address.


  • Stratum Hostname/Location

  • We have stratum servers located in the US. EU servers coming soon. Choose the one closest to your location.
  • stratum+tcp://erg.miningcrypto.live
  • Low Diff Port 3194 | Small Rigs
  • Med Diff Port 3196 | 4-6 GPU Rigs
  • For Miner Security we offer SSL Connection on port 3194 & 3196
  • stratum+ssl://erg.miningcrypto.live:port
  • High Diff Port 3100 | Nicehash & MRR Rentals
  • High Diff Port: stratum+tcp://erg.miningcrypto.live


  • Mining Algorithm

  • Ergo is a GPU mineable cryptocurrency. Ergo uses Autolykos v.2 - a Proof-of-Work algorithm designed to be ASIC resistant. Miners have to perform memory-hard computations~(at least 2.5 GB memory is needed, but the current most efficient implementation utilizes around 4 GB of vRAM) that makes Ergo friendly for GPU mining.


  • Setup Your Ergo Miner

  • Choose and download your mining software below. You can copy the sample command lines below to start your miner. You need to change the part where it says ERGO_WALLET_ADDRESS to your own ERGO wallet address and WORKER_NAME to your own worker name. You can use the worker name to track multiple miners under your address.
    •  
  • lolMiner [AMD][DevFee 1.5%] | Download Link

    lolMiner.exe --algo AUTOLYKOS2 --pool stratum+ssl://erg.miningcrypto.live:3194 --user ERGO_WALLET_ADDRESS.WORKER_NAME

  • SRBMiner-Multi [AMD] [DevFee 2.0%] | Download Link

    SRBMiner-MULTI.exe --disable-cpu --algorithm autolykos2 --pool stratum+ssl://erg.miningcrypto.live:3194 --wallet ERGO_WALLET_ADDRESS --password WORKER_NAME

  • T-Rex v0.21.0+ [NVIDIA][DevFee 2.0%] | Download Link

    t-rex -a autolykos2 -o stratum+ssl://erg.miningcrypto.live:3194 -u ERGO_WALLET_ADDRESS.WORKER_NAME -p x
